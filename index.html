Our Packages

3–4 Hour Tour

Route: Kotor – Perast – Our Lady of the Rocks (Gospa) – Panorama
Price: $79 per person • $99 All-Inclusive
Optional Cable Car: + $30 per person

Kotor: Kotor is a small town full of its magic. It will take you back through time, to when it was first called Acruvium (168 BC) or Cattaro (14th century). You’ll walk the very same streets where old Kotor citizens roamed, observing many ancient churches and monuments with stories and legends.

Perast: Driving by the coast, you will reach Perast, a small town not far from Kotor that has kept its full historical look without modernizing. Perast has two islands: St. George and the Lady of the Rocks (an artificial island). Every year, people from across Boka Bay sail to the island to throw rocks into the sea, keeping it intact and alive.

4–5 Hour Tour

Route: Kotor – Budva – Panorama Jaz – Panorama Budva
Free Time: Explore the city or enjoy a coffee break
Price: $99 per person • All-Inclusive

This route offers a blend of coastal scenery and old-town exploration. After seeing Kotor, you’ll discover Budva’s vibrant atmosphere, check out Jaz beach from a panoramic viewpoint, and soak in breathtaking views of Budva’s coast. Perfect if you want a slightly longer trip without going too far inland.

6–7 Hour Tour

Route: Kotor – Njeguši – Cetinje – Budva – Panorama
Optional Cable Car: + $30 per person
Price: $129 per person • $99 All-Inclusive (depending on add-ons)

This tour goes beyond the coast and visits the heart of Montenegro’s history and scenery. Begin in Kotor, drive up to the traditional village of Njeguši for local specialties, pass through Cetinje (the old royal capital), and end along Budva’s panoramas. For a complete Montenegrin experience!
